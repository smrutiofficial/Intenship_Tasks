    what's interesting about dogs?

    Jealousy in the Air!

    Dogs, like humans, can experience jealousy, especially when they feel their owner's attention is divided or focused on another pet or person.

    Running in the Circles

    When dogs run in circles, it can be due to excitement, playfulness, or even a way to release pent-up energy.

    Most Popular Breed in the US

    The Labrador Retriever has been the most popular dog breed in the United States for many years.

    Lower Your Blood Pressure

    Studies have shown that interacting with dogs can reduce stress and anxiety, leading to overall cardiovascular benefits and a longer, healthier life.

    The Oldest Dog Ever

    The oldest dog on record was an Australian Cattle Dog named Bluey, who lived for 29 years and 5 months.

    Superdog!

    "Superdog" refers to dogs with exceptional abilities or those trained to perform extraordinary tasks. This can include police dogs, search and rescue dogs.
